THIS IS HOW WE DO STRIPES AT QUEEN BEE

Posted on June 14, 2017 in Discover Your Style | 0 comments


We're excited to release our new chic stripe collection with so many styling options for your pregnancy. When the top and skirt are worn together it gives the illusion of wearing a dress - wear the skirt over the top or under. The Jenina Top is also a nursing top with a cropped top layer that you simply lift up to feed discreetly.


The Haidee Skirt is made of a beautiful soft fabric and the jersey waistband can be worn over the belly for more support or folded under-bump. Extend your pregnancy wardrobe further and wear the skirt with a white shirt for work or a soft cream jumper for weekends. The stylish Jenina top also looks great with jeans or trousers for work.


What we love about the Haidee Skirt is that you can wear it lower under the bump so it falls past your knees transforming the look into an instant midi-dress.
